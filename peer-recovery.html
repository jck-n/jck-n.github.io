Peer Recovery Coach

Peer Recovery Coaches are coaches that have gone through recovery and have been trained and supervised, and are here to aide your in your journey to recovery by helping you set goals and a path to recovery to return to your normal life.


Peer recovery support focuses on long-term recovery and is rooted in a culture of hope, health, and wellness. The focus of long-term peer recovery support goes beyond the reduction or elimination of symptoms to encompass self-actualization, community and civic engagement, and overall wellness. The unique relationship between the peer recovery coach and the individual in or seeking recovery is grounded in trust, and focused on providing the individual with tools, resources, and support to achieve long-term recovery.

Recovery Coach Services

Some services provided by peer recovery coaches, are forms of peer support. It involves non-clinical assistance provided to you to support your long-term recovery from substance use. The lived experience of recovery, combined with training and supervision, will assist you in initiating and maintaining your path to recovery.


By walking side by side with you in your journey to recovery, you'll receive help to create your own recovery plan and pathway.

You'll be provided with:

  • A unique relationship grounded in trust to aide in your recovery
  • A clear path and your own personalized plan to recovery
  • Aide with your personal goals inside and outside of recovery
